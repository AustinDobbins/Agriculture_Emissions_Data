Calculations to create unit of measure of greenhouse gas emission amount

- For CH4 the amount has been multiplied by 25 to compensate for the EPA's calculation determining CH4 has a 25 times more potent global warming effect when compared to CO2
- For N2O the amount has been multiplied by 298 to compensate for the EPA's calculation determining N2O has as 298 times more potent global warming effect when compared to CO2
